📚 Exam Sections at a Glance

Section 1 — ~22%

Designing Data Processing Systems

Architect for security, reliability, portability, and clean migrations.

  • IAM, org policies, CMEK & PII handling
  • Reliability: Dataform/Dataflow/Data Fusion, ACID, DR
  • Portability, data residency & multi-cloud
  • Migrations: BigQuery DTS, DMS, Datastream, Transfer Appliance

Ingesting & Processing the Data

The biggest slice — pipeline design, batch vs streaming, orchestration.

  • Sources/sinks, transformation & orchestration logic
  • Dataflow/Beam, Dataproc/Spark, Pub/Sub, Data Fusion, Kafka
  • Windowing & late-arriving data; AI data enrichment
  • Orchestration with Cloud Composer & Workflows; CI/CD

💡 Coach's Study Tip!

Sections 1–3 are two-thirds of the exam. Get the service-selection decision matrix rock-solid first (BigQuery vs Bigtable vs Spanner vs Cloud SQL; Dataflow vs Dataproc vs Data Fusion) — it's the spine of most scenario questions. Then drill streaming windowing (tumbling vs hopping vs session) and the migration tools, which examiners love to swap around. Save FinOps and reservations for a final-day skim.
