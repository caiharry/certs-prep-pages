💡 Coach's Study Tip!

This is the broadest GCP exam — don't try to know everything deeply. Instead: read all four case studies cold (many questions hang off them), internalise the Well-Architected Framework pillars as a decision lens, and drill the service-selection trade-offs (compute: Cloud Run vs GKE vs Compute Engine; storage: object vs file vs DB; DR tiers). When two answers both 'work', pick the one that best honours the stated business constraint — that's the Architect mindset.
