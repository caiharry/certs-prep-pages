How to use this portal

Every certification has the same resources in one consistent revision style: a Study Portal (exam sections with full multi-page study guides), a slide-based Cheatsheet, and two closed-book simulators — Generated Practice (original questions covering the whole exam guide) and Official Sample Questions (Google's own samples, where available).

Generated Practice = original exam-style questions written for this portal. Official Sample Questions = Google's published samples; greyed-out items are still to be added.
