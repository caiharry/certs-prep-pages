Section 5~11%

Supporting Compliance

Map regulatory and industry requirements onto Google Cloud controls — a small, high-yield slice.

Shared responsibility & regionalisation

Know the shared responsibility model (Google secures the infrastructure; you secure configuration, identity, and data). Enforce data residency with Assured Workloads and the resource-location org policy. Use Access Transparency (logs of Google access) and Access Approval (you approve Google access) for regulated estates.

Mapping compliance to controls

Translate requirements (e.g., SOC 2, PCI, data sovereignty) into concrete controls: network/access segmentation, audit-log coverage, encryption with CMEK, and least-privilege IAM. Determine which parts of the environment are in scope for each regulation.

🧠 Warm-Up Quiz

Q1. Regulated workload must run only in EU regions with controls to support compliance. Which service?

Reveal answer

Assured Workloads.

Enforces data residency, personnel, and control constraints for compliance regimes.

Q2. You must approve any Google support access to your data. Which feature?

Reveal answer

Access Approval (with Access Transparency).

Requires explicit customer approval and logs Google access.
