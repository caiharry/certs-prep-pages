Before You Begin
Read the following before starting your timed exam.
  • ⏱
    Timer

    The exam timer starts when you click Start Exam. The exam auto-submits when time expires.

  • 🏁
    Flag for Review

    Click the flag icon to mark a question for later. Flagged questions are highlighted in the review panel. Flags do not affect your score.

  • 🖰
    Eliminate an Answer

    Right-click any answer option to cross it out. Right-click again to remove the strikethrough. Eliminations do not affect your score.

  • 📝
    Personal Notes

    A notes box appears below each question. Type anything you like — notes are for your reference only and are not submitted or scored.

  • ✅
    Scoring

    Only your selected answer counts toward your score. Flags, notes, and crossed-out options have no effect on the result.

  • 🔢
    Review Panel

    The grid on the right shows all questions. Click any number to jump to that question. Answered and flagged questions are colour-coded.
